What is Grand Velora RP?

Grand Velora RP is a realistic online multiplayer roleplay game for mobile where players can take on careers, join gangs, and shape their city life.

Our Goal

We aim to provide the most immersive and fun RP experience on mobile, with fair rules, admin systems, and a strong in-game economy.

Game Features

  • Live chat and emotes
  • Player ID system
  • Starter tasks like Driver’s License
  • Join one of four unique gangs
